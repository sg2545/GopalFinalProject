"Landslide"

Dear Diary,

     I moved away from all I've ever known and am starting over. Stevie Nicks wrote “Landslide” thinking about her home and her romantic relationship with Lindsey Buckingham. Throughout the song, she is afraid of the change to come but acknowledges that it is unavoidable as time continues to pass. The twinge of the guitar and the raw vocals creates melancholic feelings towards the past and uncertain feelings towards the future. With the love I found in the previous entry, this is a new phase in our life together. Our lives stop being solely about us as individuals and start being about us both. It is anxiety inducing, but we eventually start a family and grow older together.
