"Landslide"

Dear Diary, I moved away and am starting over. Stevie Nicks wrote “Landslide” thinking about her home and her romantic relationship with Lindsey Buckingham. Throughout the song, she is afraid of the change to come but acknowledges that it is unavoidable as time continues to pass. The twinge of the guitar and the rawness of the vocals create a melancholic feeling towards the past and the future. With the love you found in the previous entry, this is a new phase in your life together as your lives stop being solely about you and start being about you both. It is anxiety inducing but you eventually start a family and grow older together.
