"Slipping Through My Fingers"

Dear Diary,

     I just said goodbye. “Slipping Through My Fingers” is written from the perspective of a mother watching her child grow up. The lyrics are infused with love for a daughter, and the sadness of watching her leave to live her own life. The chorus builds as more voices join in mirroring the building of emotion. The song also asks rhetorical questions over and over again which contributes to the feeling of writing in a diary. As this last song ends with the sound of a ticking clock in the background, my daughter starts her own clock and this story starts over again, but this time, in her own diary. This playlist then becomes her playlist.
