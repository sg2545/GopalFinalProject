"Slipping Through My Fingers"

As we experience heartbreak, fear, joy, apprehension, melancholy, and more, we contribute to our own unique stories and write page after page. A diary is an anthropological relic of who someone is, and each new entry is evidence of us learning and growing. As you continue to live, and continue to write, remember the pages behind and the pages to come. When the clock finally runs out and your diary’s last page is covered in ink, all you can hope for is to be happy with who you have become from who you once were, and to be able to flip back through your pages with pride.
