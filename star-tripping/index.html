"star tripping"

Dear Diary, I thought they loved me and I’ve never felt this before. The playlist starts with “star tripping” by Kevin Atwater, who tells the story of his first relationship. The queerness of their relationship is war with Atwater’s partner’s religious faith. This song is young and innocent, mirrored by the light tone of Atwater’s voice and the fast pace. The youthful naivety of Atwater’s partner mixed with internalized homophobia is what allows him to hurt Atwater, in what feels like for the first major time. The song feels very seventeen, where you are learning how to be your own person, but still cannot escape the grasp of parents and authority.
