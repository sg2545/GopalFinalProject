"Ribs"

 Dear Diary, college and being a young adult isn’t what I thought it would be. “Ribs” by Lorde, is a gut wrenching song of nostalgia, where all she, and by extension you, wants is to be a child once again. Reminiscent of childhood sleepovers and laughter, Lorde faces the overwhelming loneliness of post high school life. This song has minimal background noise, the simple pitch drones in the background induce a feeling of anxiety, possibly caused by unwanted change, like moving to college and leaving everything familiar behind. This is a diary entry written after a night out with people who you hoped would be your friends but turns out that they are nothing but strangers.
